F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Should the doodle be digital ?

    It can be either digital or hand drawn, we accept all kinds of doodles, which are in .jpg or .pdf format!

    (check the google form for other detials)

  • When is the last day of submissions?

    it's 15-May-2020.

  • Should the doodle include MUSE logo?

    Yes!, but you can also just use the letters in the Club name - M-U-S-E

  • Can i give my pencil sketch as entry?

    No! it should be a doodle, and as long as it is a doodle, you can make it with anything!

  • How do you decide the winner?

    On the last day of the contest, we put doodles of all submissions as story with a poll (on our instagram handle), and we keep them for 24 hrs! the poll results and the number of people vote for, is the deciding factor. Our core team will let you know the winner after the stories end!

  • What is the prize for winners?

    Ushhh!!............... We keep this thing as secret!

  • Is this a Fund rising event?

    Yes! the funds raised through this contest would be used as donations for "PM CARES FUND"
